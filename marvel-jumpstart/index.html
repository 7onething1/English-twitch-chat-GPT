Solo Power is a transparent estimate = a deck's payoff ceiling + its consistency (both shown as bars). It rates a theme on its own; the pairing engine handles how two combine. Note: a theme's rarity reflects how replayable/splashy it is, not its power level — so it doesn't drive these scores. This is a heuristic, not an official ranking (no community tier list exists yet for this June 2026 set).
✕

How pairing is scored. Match % = 50% mechanical synergy + 30% combined solo power + 20% mana consistency (mono-colour pairs are smoothest). Shared mechanics and complementary combos raise synergy; a few themes actively fight (e.g. "attack alone" vs "go wide").

Theme names, colours, rarities and mechanics from the official set, which adds two keywords: Power-Up (mana-heavy activated abilities with game-winning payoffs) and Teamwork (tap your creatures to help pay for big spells — rewards going wide). No competitive tier list exists yet for this June 2026 set, so Power/synergy values are an editable heuristic model — tweak the THEMES array and synergy rules at the top of the source.
